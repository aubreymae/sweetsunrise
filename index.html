I'm Aubrey Garcia, a game designer, technical artist, and developer based in Toronto, Canada.

I graduated with Honours from the Digital Media program at York University in 2018. Since then, I've worked in small desktop and mobile design projects and have developed a few personal games.

PROJECTS

Swipe vs. Touch Gestures

Done as a Mobile UI Research, the project aims to compare the impact of swipe and touch gestures on game user performance while playing a memory-matching card game.

K3C

A 2D vertical scroller game where you become the abandoned alley cat trying to climb its way up a dangerous building, fighting dogs and other foes along the way.

North Bear

A virtual reality puzzle game set in the Arctic North where players take on the role of a polar bear trying to survive a changing landscape affected by pollution. Collect food and navigate safely in order to make it to your pups!
